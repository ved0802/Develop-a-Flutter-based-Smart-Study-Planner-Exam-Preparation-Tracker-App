Develop a Flutter-based Smart Study Planner & Exam Preparation Tracker App that helps students plan 
subjects, schedule study sessions, track syllabus completion, and analyze preparation progress with offline-
first functionality and synchronized updates. 
Students preparing for exams often face difficulties such as: 
• 
Poor time management and unstructured study plans 
• 
Difficulty tracking syllabus completion 
• 
Lack of visibility into preparation progress 
• 
Overlooking important subjects or topics 
• 
Dependency on internet-based tools 
To address these challenges, a mobile application is required that enables students to plan study schedules, 
track completion status, and analyze preparation progress effectively, even in offline scenarios. 
Design and implement a mobile application that: 
• 
Allows students to plan study schedules 
• 
Tracks subject-wise and topic-wise progress 
• 
Provides insights into preparation status 
• 
Helps in prioritizing subjects based on completion 
• 
Supports offline usage with later synchronization 
Functional Requirements 
1. Subject & Topic Management Module 
• 
Add subjects with: 
o Subject Name 
• 
Add topics under each subject: 
o Topic Name 
o Estimated study time 
2. Study Scheduling Module 
• 
Schedule study sessions with: 
o Subject 
o Topic 
o Date & Time 
o Duration 
3. Progress Tracking Module 
• 
Mark topics as: 
o Not Started 
o In Progress 
o Completed 
• 
Track percentage completion per subject 
4. Study Dashboard 
• 
Display: 
o Total subjects 
o Completed topics 
o Pending topics 
o Daily study progress 
• 
Visual indicators (progress bars/charts) 
5. Priority & Planning Logic 
• 
Highlight: 
o Subjects with lowest completion 
• 
Suggest next topics to study (basic logic) 
6. Search & Filter Module 
• 
Search topics by name 
• 
Filter by: 
o Subject 
o Status (Completed / Pending) 
o Date 
7. Offline Functionality 
• 
Allow scheduling and tracking without internet 
• 
Store data locally 
• 
Sync updates when online 
8. Validation & Error Handling 
• 
Prevent invalid schedules (past dates optional check) 
• 
Validate required fields 
• 
Provide meaningful alerts
